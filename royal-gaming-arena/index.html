Blackjack Rules

The goal is to beat the dealer by having cards that total as close to 21 as possible without going over.

  • Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10 points each
  • Aces can be worth 1 or 11 points (whichever is more favorable)
  • If your first two cards total 21, you have a "blackjack"
  • The dealer must hit until they have at least 17 points

Mines Rules

The goal is to uncover as many gems as possible without hitting a mine.

  • Select the number of mines on the grid (more mines = higher potential multiplier)
  • Click on tiles to reveal them
  • If you reveal a gem, your multiplier increases
  • If you reveal a mine, you lose your bet
  • You can cash out at any time to secure your winnings

Coin Flip Rules

A simple game of chance where you bet on the outcome of a coin flip.

  • Choose your bet amount
  • Select either Heads or Tails
  • If you guess correctly, you double your bet
  • If you guess wrong, you lose your bet

Stock Market Rules

Predict whether the market will go up or down!

  • Choose your bet amount and select a direction (Up or Down)
  • The market will move between +100% and -100%
  • If you correctly predict the direction, you win a percentage of your bet equal to the final market movement
  • For example, if you bet 100 points on "Up" and the market ends at +75%, you win 75 points
  • If you predict incorrectly, you lose your entire bet
